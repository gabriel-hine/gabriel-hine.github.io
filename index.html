About me

I'm a signal/image processing engineer specializing in biometric systems.
I currently work at Smart Eye as a Senior R&D Engineer and Technology Owner for Iris Recognition, with responsibility for the overall technology, system architecture, and algorithmic direction. My work focuses on defining system-level trade-offs, shaping image processing and pattern recognition approaches, and ensuring performance across real-world operating conditions.
Earlier in my career, I also worked on the optical side of iris-based systems. In that context, I filed four patents on optics-based liveness detection (pending).
My background includes a PhD focused on Biometric Cryptosystems. My PhD thesis, "Anonymous Biometrics", was awarded the EAB Best Biometric Research 2020.
Although biometrics has been a core theme since my Bachelor's degree, I have also worked in orthogonal domains—one of the strengths of signal processing—including advanced ultrasonic beamforming and the analysis of cross-platform social media attacks. Selected topics are discussed in the blog.
